The Pepé Le Stew Recipe

The classic for when you don't feel like hitting the store on the way home.
Hit one of these instead! not only do they spend their whole lives
basting in a wonderful natural aroma, they're also free!

What You'll Need

  • 2 adult skunks
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 2 cups celery; finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup parsley; finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic; finely minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 carrots; chopped
  • 2 tbsp. flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 1 lb. ripe red tomatoes; peeled, seeded & chopped
  • juice of half-lemo
  • 1/4 tsp. nutmeg
  • 1 cup Madeira wine

The Mix

  1. Deskin, debone, & shred the skunks(make sure to save the scent sacs)
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Brown the skunk in a skillet
  4. Add the other stuff until lightly browned
  5. Cover, and bake in oven for three hours
  6. Strain juices and bring to a boil in separate pot
  7. Puncture scent sacs and add fluids to mixture, simmer for five mins
  8. Dunk the skunk in the sauce and enjoy!
